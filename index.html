About the game

The Scandi Quiz is an online quiz game about the Scandinavian countries and its culture. Hopefully you'll find the game a lot of fun, and maybe you learn something new!

How to play:

A Question will be displayed, choose an answer from the 4 options by clicking on the one you think is right.
If your answer is correct it will turn the answer green, and giving you a score.
If your answer is incorrect, the answer you chose will turn red, and the right answer will reveal it self.
To continue to the next question, click the next button, you need to choose an answer to be able to continue on with the quiz.

On the top of the question box you can view your score, and also which question you're on.

To go back to the homepage, simply click the text The Scandi Quiz, but doing this will remove any current score and start the quiz from the begining.
